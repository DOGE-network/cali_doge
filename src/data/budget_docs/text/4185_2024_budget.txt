PROGRAM DESCRIPTIONS 
3940 - CALIFORNIA SENIOR LEGISLATURE 
The objectives of the California Senior Legislature include: 
• Identifying priority senior concerns, 
• Developing legislative proposals in response to those concerns, and 
• Advocating for the inclusion of those concerns in legislative proposals of the State Legislature.
