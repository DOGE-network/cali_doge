3850   Coachella Valley Mountains Conservancy 
The mission of the Coachella Valley Mountains Conservancy is to acquire and hold as open space the mountainous lands 
surrounding the Coachella Valley and the natural lands within the Coachella Valley. In addition, the Conservancy works to 
protect wildlife and enhance recreational and educational experiences on those lands, to conserve local water resources and to 
promote sustainability and climate change adaptability within the desert and surrounding mountain areas.
